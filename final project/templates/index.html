About

Hello! I'm Nelson Michael, a BSc Computer Science graduate currently seeking new job opportunities. I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Django, and Python, which I've gained through my academic studies and personal projects. My passion for web development and programming started during my time at university, where I was introduced to HTML and CSS. Since then, I've expanded my skills to include JavaScript, Django, and Python, allowing me to create dynamic, interactive, and robust web applications. I'm a quick learner and a problem solver, always ready to take on new challenges and continuously improve my skills. I believe that my strong technical background and eagerness to learn make me a strong candidate for any role in the tech industry. If you have any opportunities or if you're interested in learning more about me, please feel free to get in touch. I'm excited to see where my career in tech will take me next!
